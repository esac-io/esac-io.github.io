Introduction

Well, everyone knows how hard writing can be (even more challenging using a foreign language), so this blog was created as an attempt to improve this skill and as a side effect: add a little bit more information on the data sea called VIRTUALITY.

Other goals: having an online presence and an easy platform for publishing, avoiding the usual self-sabotage. Beware though, blog posts are just daydreams from a troubled, disruptive and lazy mind, nothing more.

Virtuality

Oh yes, the 'artificial' world that surrounds our existence is growing at exponential rates. Virtual interfaces already are the main bridge used to access the present, understand the past, unveil the future, all beings, all things, interlinked.

With the decline and eminent collapse of western civilization, a clear horizon is blossoming, its almost palpable, alive, breathing, expanding, taking form, nothing and everything at the same time, existential detachment, history in the making, a unstoppable force that will obliterate every obsolete obstacle in its path.

The inevitable question: How disposable am I?
